Class


Archer

The "Archer" class is one of the most popular classes in many roleplaying and fantasy games. Archers are experts in ranged combat, skilled at hitting precise targets with bows and arrows. They are agile, stealthy and possess great dexterity, which allows them to be efficient in avoiding melee attacks and attacking from a distance with great precision.

Typical archer skills and characteristics include:

Precision Shooting: Archers are known for their ability to aim accurately at distant targets. They can hit weak spots and deal significant damage even at long range.
Agility: Agility is one of the main characteristics of an archer. They are able to move quickly and easily, making them difficult to reach in combat.
Stealth: Many archers are masters in the art of stealth, being able to hide in the shadows and attack their enemies in sneaky and surprising ways.
Ranged Weapon Specialization: Archers are experts in the use of bows and arrows, but can also be proficient with other ranged weapons such as crossbows and javelins.
High Dexterity: Their dexterity is one of the key traits that allow them to hit targets with such precision. They have quick reflexes and are able to perform complex movements with ease.
Melee Vulnerability: Despite their prowess in ranged combat, archers are generally weaker in melee combat. They prefer to keep their distance from more powerful and dangerous enemies.
Special Weapons: In addition to traditional bows and arrows, some archers can use special arrows with magical effects, such as poisoned, explosive, or enchanted arrows.
Perception Focus: Perception is an important ability for archers, as it allows them to detect enemies from a distance and anticipate enemy movements.
