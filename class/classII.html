Class



Hunter

The Hunter is a versatile and agile class in the roleplaying world, known for its tracking skills, precision and mastery of ranged weapons. Hunters specialize in hunting and eliminating targets from afar, whether using bows and arrows, crossbows or even firearms in more modern environments. With a deep connection to nature and their incredible ability to camouflage themselves, they are masters of moving silently through the most challenging terrain.
The Hunter's main feature is its tracking ability, which allows it to locate enemies and prey even in the most adverse conditions. Your keen senses and knowledge of creatures' habitats make it possible to avoid ambushes and surprise opponents. Additionally, their accuracy and dexterity with ranged weapons give them a significant advantage in combat.
Hunters can play different roles in an adventuring party. They can be excellent explorers, able to guide the group safely through unknown territories. In battle, they are skilled marksmen, capable of taking out long-range targets before they even get close enough to pose a threat.
Among his most notable abilities are True Shot, a powerful blow that deals critical damage to the target, and Rapid Shot, which lets you fire multiple arrows in quick succession. Additionally, Hunters often have the ability to tame wild animals, making them faithful companions on their journeys.
However, despite their proficiency in ranged attacks, Hunters can be vulnerable in melee combat. For this reason, they often prefer to keep a safe distance from enemies and use their camouflage and evasion abilities to avoid direct confrontation.

Mage

The Mage is an iconic and powerful class in the RPG world, known for its proficiency in magic manipulation and elemental control. Mages are scholars and sages, dedicating their lives to studying arcane mysteries and honing their magical skills. They have a unique connection to the mystical forces of the universe, allowing them to cast spells and enchantments that defy the laws of nature.
The Mage's main feature is his ability to cast spells. They can conjure devastating flames, summon powerful storms, heal wounds with healing magic, and much more. Their magical versatility allows them to adapt to different situations and challenges, making them essential in many adventuring parties.
In addition to their magical combat skills, Mages are also known for their vast knowledge of alchemy, potions, and enchantments. They can create magic items and potions that grant party members unique benefits, empowering them to face powerful enemies.
However, mastery of magic comes with a price. Mages generally have a frailer build and are less skilled in hand-to-hand combat. They must depend on your cunning, intelligence and strategy to stay safe in dangerous situations. Furthermore, manipulating magic requires great concentration and continuous study, which can make Mages more vulnerable in prolonged combat or in situations where they are prevented from casting their spells.
Among the Mages' most impressive abilities are area spells, which allow them to target multiple enemies with a single spell, and illusion spells, which can deceive and confuse opponents. Additionally, Mages often have access to teleportation and other locomotion magic, allowing them to quickly travel the world and explore places inaccessible to others.
