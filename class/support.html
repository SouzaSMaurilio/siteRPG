Class


Support

Support is one of the most important and strategic classes in RPGs. Your role is to provide assistance and support to the group, ensuring that other team members have the ideal conditions to excel in combat. While other classes focus on dealing damage or defending, Support focuses on healing wounds, providing buffs and debuffs, as well as offering magical or strategic support.
One of Support's main abilities is healing, allowing them to restore hit points to injured party members during or after a battle. They may also have access to area healing spells, capable of treating multiple limbs simultaneously. In addition, Support is able to remove negative statuses such as poison or curses, restoring health and efficiency to allies.
Another crucial function of Support is to provide buffs to improve the party's capabilities. They can temporarily increase teammates' strength, agility, defense, or other attributes, making them more powerful in combat. These buffs are essential for facing more powerful enemies or overcoming difficult challenges.
In addition to benefiting allies, Support can also debilitate enemies by applying debuffs that reduce their effectiveness in combat. They can weaken the enemy's stamina, slow it down, or impair its ability to attack. These skills can be crucial for weakening powerful bosses or avoiding dangerous attacks.
Other Support abilities can include the ability to resurrect fallen allies, provide magical or physical protection, and even manipulate the battlefield to favor the party. Some Supports may also be experts at crowd control spells, capable of freezing, petrifying, or incapacitating groups of enemies.
However, Support also faces challenges of its own. They generally have less direct attack abilities and can be vulnerable in one-on-one combat. Therefore, they depend on the protection of other group members to continue providing effective support.
