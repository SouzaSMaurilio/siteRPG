Class



Tanker

The Tanker class is one of the most important and fundamental classes in RPG games. Its main role is to act as a human shield, absorbing damage from enemies and protecting the weakest members of the party. This class is essential to maintain stability and balance in battles, allowing other team members to focus on dealing damage to enemies.

Tanker Class Features:

High Resistance: Tankers are known to possess a high level of physical and magical resistance. They have a large amount of hit points and a high resistance to physical and magical attacks, which makes them capable of withstanding powerful blows from enemies.
Protection Abilities: Tankers have special abilities that allow them to protect allies from enemy attacks. This includes self-defense skills such as blocking and dodging, as well as area protection skills to reduce damage dealt to the entire party.
Agro Management: Tankers have the ability to draw and sustain aggression from enemies, causing them to focus their attacks on the tank instead of attacking more vulnerable allies. This skill is known as "agro management" and is critical to keeping the team safe.
Armor and Shields: Tankers often wear heavy armor and shields to further increase their defense. These pieces of equipment provide additional strength and make the tanker even more efficient in their role of protecting the group.
Taunt: The taunt skill is one of the trademarks of tankers. It allows the tanker to draw enemies' attention to himself, making them attack with priority. This is especially useful when enemies are attacking other party members or when you need to reposition the enemy during battle.
Leadership and Strategy: In addition to their combat skills, tankers often act as group leaders, coordinating actions and strategies during battles. Your ability to make quick and efficient decisions is crucial to your team's success.
Versatility: While tankers specialize in defense, many of them also have controlled damage or crowd control abilities. These skills allow them to keep enemies in check and be a more complete force on the battlefield.

Shaman

The Shaman class is a mystical and spiritual figure present in many roleplaying games. With origins in ancient cultures and shamanic beliefs, this character has a deep connection with nature and spirits, which grants him unique abilities and supernatural powers.
The Shaman is often portrayed as a healer and protector, able to channel energies to heal wounds, purify souls, and strengthen his allies during battle. In addition, he has knowledge of medicinal herbs, potions and rituals, which makes him a respected figure sought after by those seeking wisdom and spiritual help.
His connection to nature also grants him the ability to call on the powers of the elements, such as fire, water, earth and air, to damage enemies and manipulate the environment around him. By interacting with the spirits of animals and ancestors, the Shaman can even assume the form of animals, gaining their abilities and special characteristics.
On the battlefield, the Shaman can play a strategic role, supporting his team with healing and blessings, or even casting curses and enchantments to weaken opponents. This versatility makes it a unique and valuable class in an adventuring team, able to adapt to different situations and challenges.
