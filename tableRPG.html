History

For a long time, civilizations were not yet populous and the lands of the kingdom were not explored, but with the growth of races, the kingdom became known for its adventurers.
This custom of adventurers exploring the lands in search of gold, glory and adventures, was born with a young Halfling who decided to take a risk in the world beyond the mountains his name was Dmikla so'Sotura, this young man started to walk through the lands of the kingdom meeting Humans, Orcs, Fairies and Elves, he was the one who designed the map of the kingdom and who sang the first songs of battles against dragons, sea serpents and other creatures.
Over time, inspired by the stories of this adventurer, others took courage and left to explore the world.
Humans with a map in hand began to create roads and streets to connect their lands, and then came the first dispute over lands known as the great war, and in that a powerful wizard created the Bestials, after the great war the nations were delimited, everyone knew where the lands of the Elves, humans, fairies and the like were, but for some reason the number of beings and new species of creatures that emerge annually is alarming and thanks to that, adventurers and hunters are still a necessity, and there are always groups of people who decide walk your life from village to village killing monsters in exchange for gold, food and drink.
