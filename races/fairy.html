Fairys

History of Fairys: According to legend, the fairies were the first beings of the Kingdom, and when they appeared everything was desert, they used their magic to create forests, rivers, mountains and lakes, they made the whole kingdom unite and transform, until it had the shape of the current kingdom, with its cities, its animals, races and beings.








Caracteristicas: The mystical Fairies, a race of winged beings, are small, averaging 20 cm in height. despite their size, fairies are strong beings, with a great connection to magic, they can manipulate nature to her will, there are reports of a fairy who made a tree grow in the mouth of a Troll, killing him instantly, but Rumors aside, these creatures are beautiful and have insect-like wings, live in large (for their proportions) villages that can contain up to 50,000 of them.
For a long time they were hunted because it was believed that their wings increased the magical powers of those who consumed them.

Habits

Faeries are hospitable and bond well with all races these days, and so they welcome everyone in need into their community, but not for long. Despite the hunt for fairies during the great war, they remain faithful to what they believe, that all beings are parts of a whole. Every summer the fairies throw a big party for the universe thanking them for the gift of life and everything that surrounds them.
Their cities are shaped like large hives that are buried in the earth, despite the size of the fairies, the tunnels of these hives are on average 1.5 m high, which facilitates the entry of beings of other races.
