Humans


History of the Humans:

This is the most common race in the kingdom, its members are spread across almost every country. The first citation about humans was before the creation of the kingdom, they say that they already inhabited tribes in forests, riverbanks, mountains, amidst glaciers and deserts. Thus proving to be an extremely resistant and adaptable breed. Over time, humans gained a reputation for being hospitable and unreliable, a reputation that continues to this day.

characteristics:

The stature of humans varies greatly from one geographic location to another, even their physical characteristics, in deserts for example humans have darker skin and are taller and more slender, while in the icy plains they have fair skin, are shorter, and have slanted corners of their elfs.
Humans are also not known for their strength, endurance, speed, or anything that stands out physically. But they say they are great military strategists and very useful in wars.
Habits:

As well as their physical characteristics, the customs of humans vary from place to place, humans in the central region of the kingdom are polytheistic, and worship several gods related to the sky, such as the moon, clouds, sun, stars and each with its own name.
Already in the frozen lands, there is only one god, who they call the creator, but there are several children of that one who are distributed throughout the world, giving humans the grace of their father. In terms of parties and events, it is rare for two tribes or cities of humans to celebrate the same dates, for the same issues, in large cities it is common to see parties for the creator of the city, or for its kings, princes and heroes, while in villages and tribes it is common to celebrate the day of fertility, day of the sun, eclipses and the like.
