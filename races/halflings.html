Halflings

History of Halflings: They emerged a long time ago from caves in the Seraph mountains in the north, considered abominations by humans, they were welcomed by the fairies who helped them adapt to the surface world, although they are currently a common race in the cities of the world, they cannot jobs because they were considered weak and sometimes because of their stature, so these creatures got used to being thieves, musicians and domestic servants.



Characteristics: They are light and silent creatures, in addition to excellent storytellers, they do not have cities of their own on the surface, only communities in caves and mountains, communities that are considered barbarians.
Halflings enjoy a great deal of fame on the black market, a market dominated by goods they acquire on a less-than-bid basis.

Habits

Halflings are gentle and playful creatures, they like to drink and sing, usually halflings are part of caravans as bards, and are often tavern owners.
The traditions of the barbarian halflings are unknown to the surface folk, as they avoid contact with them.
