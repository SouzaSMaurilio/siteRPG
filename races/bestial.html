Bestials

History of the Bestials: The beast race originates from the plains of Condir, near the southern seas, this warrior race is also known for its ability to hunt and fish. Its main cities are located in places that have a great diversity of animals and fish. Although no one knows for sure the origin of this species, it is believed that it was the work of an elven wizard during the great war, who created this race to face the hordes of orcs.

characteristics: These beings have different heights and formats, as each family has the characteristics of a specific animal, such as the bear-men who are more than two meters tall, or the beaver-men who are less than 50cm.
His main physical abilities are:

    Uncommon strength
    resistance of specific animals
    speed

Habits:

The bestials are not very polite, in fact it is rare that they invite someone who is not of their race to enter their homes, but despite this, they tolerate foreigners because their main economic source is the trade of spices, leather and fisheries.
