Orcs


History of the Orcs:

The clever Orcs are one of the most feared races in the reign, due to their cunning, they are great at ambushes, in addition to being skilled warriors, but none of this is reflected in its architectural structure or even in its words.
Orcic communication is underdeveloped and everything that depends on it, follows the same path, which generates in people alien to their culture a false notion of precariousness and low development of intelligence.

characteristics:

Orcs live in two types of structures, nomadic bands and underground cities.
Nomadic bands usually wear clothes that completely cover their body, they have an aversion to the sun and to minimize this fear, orcs wear translucent cloths over their eyes.
As for underground city-like structures, the orcs have large complexes of caverns hollowed out as if forming dome-shaped homes and long, slender buildings that connect the floor to ceiling of the main cavern of the cluster they call a city.
Habits:

The orcs worshiped the gods of darkness, night, and fire. And they value strength, their leaders are defined by their strength in combat, when an orc thinks he has the right to reign, he challenges the leader of the caravan or city to a duel to the death, the one who survives wins the right to reign, and can only be challenged to a new combat, after 13 full moons from the last one.
